# Privacy Policy for Better New Tabs

**Last Updated: December 5, 2025**

## Data Collection
"Better New Tabs" does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any user data.

## Data Usage
-   **Local Storage**: Your preferences (name, theme settings) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API and standard `localStorage`.
-   **Bookmarks**: The extension accesses your bookmarks `chrome.bookmarks` solely to display and organize them within the new tab page. This data never leaves your browser.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
